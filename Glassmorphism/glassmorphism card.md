## ⚠️ Important Tips (Most people miss this)

* Your blur **won’t show properly** if background is flat → always use gradients
* Add **padding to body** so card isn’t stuck to edges
* Try:

```css
body {
  min-height: 100vh;
  display: flex;
  justify-content: center;
  align-items: center;
}
```
